Introduction

  • The Fußball-Bundesliga,is also known as Bundesliga,it is a professional association football league in Germany and it is recognized as Germany's primary football competition.

  • It consists of 18 teams, Bundesliga season occurs during the time period of August to May and games are usually played during the weekends, Bundesliga clubs qualify for the DFB-Pokal and the winner qualifes for the DFL-Supercup.

  • La Liga is one of the most popular professional sports leagues in the world, with an average attendance of 26,933 for league matches in the 2018–19 season. This is the sixth-highest of any domestic professional sports league in the world and the third-highest of any professional association football league in the world, behind the Bundesliga and the Premier League.

  • Bayern Munich has won the most titles out of the 56 teams that were enrolled, it is considered as one of the top ranked national leagues and is currently ranked thrid in Europe according to UEFA league coeffiecient rankings during the 2019/2020 season

History

  • Bundesliga originated in 1962 in Dortmund and was founded by the Deutscher Fußball-Bund(German football association) and now its handled by the Deutsche Fußball Liga (German football league).

  • The first season began in 1963, which had to undergo many changes to perfect the league.
